Portfolio

Showcasing Your Work

A portfolio serves as a bridge between your academic projects and professional capabilities. It allows employers to see your methodology, documentation skills, and the impact of your information science solutions.

Your portfolio is a visual representation of your problem-solving process. Whether you are in UX, Data Science, or Library Science, a portfolio is essential for the MSI pathway.
