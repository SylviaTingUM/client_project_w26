Informational Interviews & The TIARA Method

Informational interviews are one of the most effective ways to learn about a company's culture and daily operations. The TIARA method provides a specific question framework to extract the most valuable insights during these conversations.

Learn how to conduct an informational interview that yields high-value insights using the TIARA method (Trends, Insights, Advice, Resources, Assignments).
